Who am I?

To many of you I am just an ordinary student. I love watching Netflix shows and YouTube videos. I hate homework. I am artistic; I like to create graphics, videos and websites (like this one). I love listening to music and singing. Lastly, I am grateful to be surrounded by amazing friends.

Why vote for me?

A vote for me will never be wasted. I come bearing a solid action plan, leadership skills and the will to serve. From expanding the scope of co-curriculars, to reducing the burden on students and putting ILS on the global map, I want to do it all. Along with my ideas, I want to hear from you. I am a good listener and believe in the ideas of ILS students. I am a talented and experienced leader as proved by my team’s successes in the WGHS Investment Competition and Toycathon among many others. Now, it is time for me to use those talents in service of you and our school. Together, let’s transform our ideas into reality.
